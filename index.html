Crystal Collectors

To begin the game a random number is automatically generated.
Start by clicking a crystal below. Each crystal is assigned a different value which is hidden until the crystal is clicked.
Once you click on the crystal you will receive a random amount of points. You win the game once the amount of points you have scored is equal to the random number.
You lose the game if the amount of points you score is greater than the random number generated.
